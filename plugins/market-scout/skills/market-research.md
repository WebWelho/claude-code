# Market Research Skill

This skill enables comprehensive market research using web search and content analysis.

## Capabilities

- Search for recent product launches and tech news
- Analyze company websites and product pages
- Cross-reference multiple sources for accuracy
- Evaluate market potential for Finnish context

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Researching a specific topic area for new developments
- Verifying whether a product is available in Finland
- Gathering competitive intelligence
- Analyzing market trends

## Research Approach

1. Start broad: search for general category news
2. Narrow down: find specific products and tools
3. Verify: check URLs and confirm information accuracy
4. Contextualize: assess relevance to Finnish market

## Tools

- WebSearch: for discovering new products and news
- WebFetch: for reading product pages, pricing, and availability details

## Best Practices

- Always check publication/launch dates — prioritize recent (last 48h)
- Verify Finland availability by checking for Finnish language support, EUR pricing, EU data centers
- Note confidence level when information is uncertain
- Cross-reference claims across multiple sources when possible
